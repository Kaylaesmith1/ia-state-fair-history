Come to Des Moines, IA &


join the fun!

The Iowa State Fair is nationally renowned and internationally recognized. Annually, for 11 days in August, visitors from all over the world come to Des Moines, Iowa to take part in the great celebration of this Midwestern state's achievement in agriculture, industry and much more. Come see for yourself!

How to get here


The Iowa State Fairgrounds lies in the heart of Des Monies, Iowa's east side. Private transportation is most commonly used to arrive at the grounds, though shuttle busses are available and run frequently from the state Capitol building.
