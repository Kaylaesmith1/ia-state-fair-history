THe Butter Cow


The first Butter Cow was created in 1911. In 1960, the first female (and first Iowan) became the fourth sculptor.


Starting with a mesh frame and ~270kg of Iowa-made butter, the sculptor works in a crisp, 4°C cooler. When finished, a life-sized cow (167cm tall, 243cm long) stands tall.


Famous Iowans molded out of butter have included Superman Returns actor Brandon Routh and olympic gymnast Shawn Johnson.

Five Fun Facts

  1. The Iowa State Fair is the largest event in the state of Iowa!
  2. The first Iowa State Fair was held in 1854, from October 25th-27th.
  3. The Fair welcomed over a million people (1,008,174!) for the first time in 2002.
  4. The hottest Fair recorded was a whopping 42°C in 1983.
  5. Only 6 fairs have been cancelled, the most recent due to Covid-19.
